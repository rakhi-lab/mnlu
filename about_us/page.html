About MNLU Mumbai

Maharashtra National Law University, Mumbai

Welcome to MNLU Mumbai

Maharashtra National Law University Mumbai is one of the premier law universities in India, offering state-of-the-art education, research facilities, and global collaborations.

Established in 2014, MNLU Mumbai has been striving to provide students with a comprehensive legal education that balances theoretical and practical aspects of law.

Our Vision

To nurture future leaders in the field of law by providing an environment that encourages research, innovation, and practical learning.
